Examples

One thing that sets Square1 apart from other sliders is the ability to either have the images fill the space or be resized so that the entirety of the image is shown without cropping. The fill_mode property will let you control how and how much of your images people see.

$('.slideshow1').square1({ fill_mode: 'cover' });
$('.slideshow1').square1({ fill_mode: 'contain' });
$('.slideshow2').square1({ height: '30vh', prev_next_nav: 'outside', dots_nav: 'outside', caption: 'inside' });
$('.slideshow3').square1({ prev_next_nav: 'outside', dots_nav: 'outside' });
$('.slideshow4').square1({ slide_duration: 100, transition_time: 1000, auto_start: false, prev_next_nav: 'none', dots_nav: 'none' }); $('.custom_next_slide').click(function() { $('.slideshow4').square1('next'); });
